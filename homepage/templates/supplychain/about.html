| About FarmConnect Supply Chain Management |


Welcome to FarmConnect, where innovation meets agriculture. FarmConnect Supply Chain Management is a cutting-edge platform dedicated to revolutionizing the agricultural supply chain, empowering farmers, distributors, and consumers alike.

|Our Mission|


At FarmConnect, we are committed to creating a more efficient, transparent, and sustainable agricultural supply chain. We strive to bridge the gap between producers and consumers, ensuring that quality produce reaches its destination seamlessly while minimizing waste and environmental impact.


| Features |


  • Interactive Chart to display current available stock
  • Stock details like current available stock are maintained
  • Incoming transactions are done through purchases
  • Outgoing transactions are done through sales
  • Every transaction performed through the software is recorded
  • Bill is dynamically rendered and can be printed
  • Separate profiles are maintained for suppliers
